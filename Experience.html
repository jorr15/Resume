Experience

A life worth living

I enjoy learning and growing outside of formal education. As you will notice as you read this section, you can be taught much beyond the classroom.

  • Track and Field

    I have had the chance to compete in Track at a very high level. In 2021 I won the National championship for high school and am now ranked in the top 100 collegiate 800 meter runners in the nation. More than the accolades, I've learned how to set goals and achieve them. I've learned how to be diligent in putting work in each day. I've learned how to set my mind to something, then work incredibly hard to achieve it.

  • Mission Experience

    Between 2021-2023, I served a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in North Carolina. I was given the assignment to learn Spanish and loved my experience. Meaning to life comes as you connect with the people and culture of those who were once strangers to you. I learned how to study, how to interact with many diverse cultures, became profecient in Spanish, and how to work for something you truly care about.

  • Work experience

    I previously worked for SMPL manfucturing which specializes in the manufacturing of beauty products. I started as an assembly line worker but do to an innate ablity to learn quickly, I was moved into the engineering department. I learned how the machines worked and was tasked with the maintenance and future development of the manufacturing lines. It was an incredible learning experience. I got to know the company inside and out.
